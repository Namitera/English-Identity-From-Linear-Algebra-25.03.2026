kept ideas that appear in the presentation:
Phase space represents identity of a person 
Vector direction is the compositional ratio of attributes 
Vector length is the overall magnitude or development amount of the identity 
Basis vector defines the available attributes as well as their strength and correlation with other attributes 
Dot product: Measurement for determining alignment of 2 people's identities, gets bigger with higher magnitudes 
Norm operator: Determines the ratio of composition 
Nd Matrix for n attributes Linear transformation as an operator that changes identity -> column space
Opposite vector shows a person who is the complete opposite of you 
Multiplication is making someone with more pronounced attributes 
Addition gives you with both of your attributes combined+ more strength if you have some correlation 
Angle: Similar to dot product, shows how far away 2 persons are but only composition wise not absolute. 
Cross products: Can easily see if 2 people have the came composition of attributes 
Used to find someone whose composition is most different from the other 2 persons 
Inverse matrix is an action reversing the original action
